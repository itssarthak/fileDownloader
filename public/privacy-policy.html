Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2024
Privacy-First Approach: FileDownloader.in processes all files directly in your browser. We never see, store, or have access to your URLs or downloaded files. Your data stays completely private on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Your Files: We never store or access files you download
  • Your URLs: URL lists are processed entirely in your browser
  • Personal Data: No registration required, no personal information collected
  • File Content: We cannot see what files you're downloading

Information We DO Collect

  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, general geographic location
  • Performance Metrics: How our tool performs to improve service quality

2. How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used only to:

  • Improve our service performance and user experience
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

3. Data Processing Location

Your Browser: All file processing happens directly in your web browser. When you upload a CSV file or paste URLs, everything is processed locally on your device.

Our Servers: We only use servers to deliver the web application itself. Your actual data never reaches our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

  • We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our tool
  • Data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users
  • You can opt out using browser settings or ad blockers
  • We comply with GDPR requirements for analytics data

CORS Proxy Services

  • When files can't be downloaded directly, we may use proxy services
  • These services only see the file URLs, not your identity
  • Proxy usage is automatic and temporary
  • We use multiple providers to ensure privacy and reliability

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Essential Cookies

  • Session storage for download progress (cleared when you close browser)
  • Temporary storage for CSV/Excel file processing
  • User preference settings (optional features)

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics cookies for usage statistics
  • Can be disabled in your browser settings
  • Do not contain personal information

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communications are encrypted
  • No Server Storage: Your files never leave your device
  • Browser Security: We rely on your browser's built-in security
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security measures current

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Request what data we have (very limited)
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of any analytics data
  • Right to Portability: Export any data we hold
  • Right to Object: Opt out of analytics tracking

Under CCPA (California Users)

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

8. International Data Transfers

Since all processing happens in your browser, there are no international data transfers of your personal files or URLs. Analytics data may be processed by Google in various countries according to their privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Notify users of significant changes via our website

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

  • Website: FileDownloader.in
  • Email: privacy@filedownloader.in
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

12. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing the limited data we collect:

  • Legitimate Interest: Improving our service and user experience
  • Consent: You consent to analytics when using our service
  • Service Delivery: Technical data necessary for the tool to function

  • ✅ No file storage on our servers
  • ✅ All processing happens in your browser
